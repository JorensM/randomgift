How it works

  1. Place and pay for an order, optionally specifying what kind of a gift you want, for example tech, beauty, toys, etc.
  2. We will review the order, and then purchase a random gift according to your selected price level and fitting your preferences from a major retailer
  3. The gift will be sent to your address

FAQ

What kinds of gifts can I receive?

When filling out your order, you will be able to specify a category or multiple categories of kinds of products which you would like to receive. Categories include:

  • Tech
  • Decor
  • Beauty
  • Handmade
  • Clothing
  • Books
  • Video-game themed toys/accessories
  • Animal toys

Additionally you will be able to specify the color of the gift and whether you want to receive multiple small gifts in 1 package or a single larger gift.

Where are you getting the gifts from?

The gifts are sourced from several major retailers such as Etsy, Amazon, Temu

Will you package the gift?

Currently we don't offer gift packaging as we send the gift straight to you. This keeps to costs down since we don't have to ship the item twice, but in the future we would like to offer an option to package your gift before sending it to you.

Are the items brand new?

Yes, we only order brand new items

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the nature of the service, we will rarely refund a gift, unless it has been found to be damaged when you received it.
